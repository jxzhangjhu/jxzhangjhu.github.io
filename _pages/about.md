I am a Research Scientist at [Salesforce AI Research](https://www.salesforceairesearch.com/), specializing in Reliable AI Agents and Foundation Model.

**Previously**: I was a Senior Staff Research Scientist and founding member, building AI Research Team at [Intuit](https://www.intuit.com/). Prior to this, I was a Staff Research Scientist in Computer Science and Mathematics Division at [Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL)](https://www.ornl.gov/). I received my Ph.D. from the [Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/).
